Create a Routine

A routine is a group of exercises you'll practice each session. You can make as many routines as you'd like, which is especially useful if you play more than one instrument or if you want to craft new routines for specific goals. My Practice Pal will help you keep track of how many times you have practiced the exercises in your routines.

Variations

The MPP log tool captures whatever level of detail you decide. When you log a music practice session, MPP will automatically give you the option to input the specific keys and modes you worked on that session, as well as the fastest tempo you could play successfully that session. The log will also automatically offer descriptors for common directional variations: "forwards", "backwards", or "forwards & backwards." In addition to the variation descriptors provided, you can add your own variation descriptions to capture the details you want to keep track of in your own words.
